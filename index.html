Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MoraEcho is an AI-assisted emotional conversation application designed for adults. We are committed to protecting user privacy and ensuring transparent data handling.

Eligibility

MoraEcho is intended for users aged 18 and above. The app is not designed for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information required to operate and improve the app, including:

  • Email address (for login and account identification)
  • Optional profile information such as display name and profile image
  • Optional voice samples provided by the user for voice features
  • Chat history created within the app
  • Device information such as OS version, performance logs, and analytics

How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used for:

  • Personalizing AI-generated responses
  • Account authentication and security
  • Feature improvement and performance enhancement
  • User analytics to understand app usage trends

AI Conversations

All chats inside MoraEcho are generated by AI models. Users are not communicating with real individuals. Chat data is stored only to provide continuity and personalization of your experience.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade user data. Some third-party services (such as analytics, crash reporting, or AI model processors) may securely process limited data solely to support app functionality.

Data Storage and Security

Data is stored securely and protected using modern security practices to prevent unauthorized access.

Account and Data Removal

Users may request deletion of their account and stored data at any time by contacting our support team.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated occasionally. Any future changes will be published on this page.
